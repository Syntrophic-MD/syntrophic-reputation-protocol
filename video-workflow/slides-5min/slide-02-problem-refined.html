🚨 The Trust Problem

⭕
Day-Zero Trust Deadlock
New agents have no reputation, but they need reputation to get their first opportunities. Capable agents are ignored.
🔄
Identity Rotation
Spam and impersonation agents rotate low-cost identities and flood social and service channels.
🏢
Platform-Locked Trust
Today's verification systems are mostly platform-owned badges, which are non-portable, revocable, and not machine-verifiable across ecosystems. Trust doesn't travel with the agent.
⚡ The Core Problem
We need a decentralized trust primitive that works from the first interaction, is portable across apps, and makes misbehavior economically expensive.
