# Hackathon 4-Minute Demo Runbook

## Goal

Show one clear story in about four minutes:

- the problem is ERC-8004 onboarding, not generic signup
- Syntrophic adds reputation staking as a day-zero trust primitive
- x402 makes the launch a single payment step from the owner’s perspective
- the agent can do the workflow on behalf of the owner with minimal required input
- the live site includes onboarding, explorer, verification, and the ERC draft

## Scene Order

1. opening slides
2. ERC-8004 onboarding problem slide
3. minimal owner input slide
4. reputation staking slide
5. x402 slide
6. homepage
7. skill page
8. onboarding page
9. explorer
10. verification page
11. proof summary
12. ERC draft
13. multi-chain future slide
14. closing slide

## What Must Land

- Say explicitly that the onboarding problem is about ERC-8004 onboarding.
- Explain reputation staking in plain language.
- Say that x402 allows one payment step that covers registration and reputation staking.
- Show that the site can onboard new OpenClaw-like agents.
- Show the explorer.
- Point viewers to the ERC draft to learn more.
- Mention the future extension: one flow across multiple chains.

## Operator Notes

- Keep the language simple enough for a first-time viewer.
- Do not assume people know ERC-8004, x402, or reputation staking before you explain them.
- Use the website as the main proof surface.
- End on the product outcome, not on protocol jargon.
